# Business Performance Sales Analytics  -  Excel Project

> A comprehensive sales and finance analytics project focused on transforming raw data into meaningful business insights through advanced Excel tools, including Power Query, Power Pivot, DAX, and data modelling, to uncover trends, evaluate performance, and support data-driven decision-making.
---

### ❗The Problem
AtliQ Hardwares struggled with inconsistent sales performance and limited visibility into profitability, revenue trends, customer contributions, and KPI tracking due to fragmented data spread across 100,000+ sales transaction records. The absence of structured reporting and consolidated insights hindered effective performance monitoring, strategic planning, and data-driven decision-making.

* Analysed and transformed 100,000+ sales transaction records across products, customers, and markets into a structured reporting framework.
* Built interactive sales and finance analytics dashboards to monitor revenue trends, target achievement, customer performance, and profitability KPIs.
* Enabled data-driven decision-making by providing consolidated business insights through advanced Excel, Power Query, Power Pivot, DAX, and data modelling techniques.
---
##  📝 Overview

AtliQ Hardwares operates in diverse global markets, with multiple products, divisions, and distributors. 

However, challenges like inconsistent sales performance, unmet targets, and opaque profitability require in-depth analysis to inform decision-making and strategic planning.

Develop a structured Excel-based analytics solution to monitor and evaluate:
